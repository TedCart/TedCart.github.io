Ted Cart

Freelance Fullstack Developer

Hello!

My name is Ted Cart and I'm a freelance full-stack web developer in the Boston area.

I discovered my passion for coding after several jobs in the financial sector. After successfully creating a spreadsheet to solve sudoku puzzles (complete with iterative guessing!) I knew I wanted to do this full-time.

I like the work, I like the people, I like who I am when I do it. The goals of any project are straight-forward, but the solution never is. People bring different strengths and ideas yet the common goal keeps the discussion grounded. Most of all I love those random moments between work hours when the answer to some complex problem just comes to you and you race the clock to get it down in writing. I completed General Assembly's web immersion program to legitimize my passion and turn it into a career.

I'm excited to engage in a productive team environment doing work I'm good at, working on projects I care about.

Technologies: JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Bootstrap, Handlebars, Express.js, MongoDB, Mongoose, Amazon Web Services

BitStash is an online storage solution that allows you to upload files you have on your personal computer to a remote server for access from anywhere in the world! Users can create an account, and privately upload personal files of any type to the cloud, where they can then access them with a web browser.

In this version, we have limited the size of a file upload to 10MB.
